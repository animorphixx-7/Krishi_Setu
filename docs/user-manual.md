# User Manual

1. **Sign up** at `/auth` with email/password or Google. Verify your email.
2. **Complete your profile** at `/profile` — district powers weather and advice.
3. **Weather** — `/weather` shows real 7-day forecast + farming hints. Cached
   for an hour; offline-safe via stale fallback.
4. **Crop recommendation** — `/crop-recommendation` asks for soil/season/water
   then returns AI-ranked crops. History is saved.
5. **Plant disease scan** — `/crop-doctor`: upload a leaf photo, get plant +
   disease, severity, and treatment.
6. **Farming advisor** — `/farming-advisor`: pick advice type (daily/weekly
   /irrigation/fertilizer), get structured tips grounded in your weather.
7. **AI chat** — `/ai-chat` for free-form questions in English/Hindi/Marathi.
8. **Equipment** — browse `/equipment`, request a booking. Contact number is
   revealed only after the booking is confirmed.
9. **Community** — share at `/community`.
10. **Government schemes** — eligibility checker at `/government-schemes`.
